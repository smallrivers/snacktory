General Electric Company (GE) Crosses The Finish Line: Completes Metem Corp Merger

A look into how the merger will help both parties enhance their operational capabilities

Published By: Ramsha Amir on April 5, 2016 08:21 am EST

General Electric Company (NYSE:GE) announced that it has completed its merger with Metem Corporation. This will help boost the company in terms of costs and providing more options available to customers.

The merger will help the company to save significant amount of costs. It will help it increase functional efficiency and at the same time will help reduce emissions.

The merger will also help enhance Metem’s capabilities and capacities; will leading to the integration of resources into General Electric. On the other hand, the merger will help GE power to further enhance its manufacturing technologies.

It was in December when General Electric announced its agreement to acquire Metem Corporation. Metem Corporation is the main provider of cooling precision and manufacturing technologies.

We believe that this acquisition is significant as it displays the company’s willingness to improve operational efficiency and profitability. The merger will help in cost savings which will eventually lead to greater profitability.

General Electric fell 2.33% as at 1:44 PM EDT, it is currently trading at $31.18. The company has a market capitalization of $290.93 billion.

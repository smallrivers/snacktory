    Allan Wu dating Malaysian beauty queen and "Amazing Race" contestant?

    The Hive AsiaJune 14, 2017


    Chinese-American hunk Allan Wu, 45 is reportedly going out with beauty queen Lee Yvonne, 29 best known for her title as Miss World Malaysia 2012.

    Though rumours has been circulating for the past few months that the two are actually an item, none of them officially confirmed it until yesterday, when Malaysia-based Lee posted a photo of her and Wu on her Instagram, celebrating his birthday.

    The photo captioned "Happy birthday, silly! Thank you for being so amazing and you deserve only the best and all the happiness in the world!", where Lee is seen lovingly sitting on Wu's lap as he wrapped his hands around her waist.

    Although neither of them have verbally confirmed their relationship yet, many people took to the photo as an obvious announcement of their coupling and have expressed their congratulations for the two.

    It is believed that that Lee and Wu has started dating sometime after "The Amazing Race Asia" Season 5, as Lee was one of the contestants on the show along with her partner, Malaysian beauty queen Chloe Chen, while Wu was the host.


    Lee and Chen placed second on "The Amazing Race Asia" after a close but intense competition.

    Singapore-based Allan Wu was formerly married to Mediacorp actress Wong-Li-Lin and have a daughter and son together. The pair divorced in 2013.

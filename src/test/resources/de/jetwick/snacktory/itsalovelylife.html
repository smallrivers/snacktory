Why Having A Sparkling Smile Is Important To Me

Why Having A Sparkling Smile Is Important To Me

I am still working my way through my New Year’s resolutions with great results this year. My most exciting resolution was to have a sparkling smile that I can be proud of and I’m so excited to share that I’ve partnered with the Invisalign® brand to make it happen!

2014 felt like the year of getting back on track…

The girls were all at different schools and I was working what felt like non-stop. We were all in a good place, but it felt like we were redefining our new normal, in a really good way. Change is never easy and that goes for setting new healthy habits too. And at the end of the day, I felt like there wasn’t much left for me to give to myself.

2015 has been totally different…

It’s all about refining our new system and enjoying all of our hard work that brought us to this point. With that came a realization that it’s not about having enough time to take care of me, but making time in my schedule that was important, so I made some resolutions at the beginning of the year and I’ve been keeping with them. I know I’m not alone in 2015 being different, because a recent survey conducted by Kelton and sponsored by the makers of Invisalign clear aligners, shows that more women are keeping their resolutions than ever this year!

I know personally that self improvement is hard and that’s why I’m excited about September being Self Improvement Month!

It’s a month to redefine where we want to be in the future and to step up our efforts. I’ve been looking back at my resolutions and some I’ve already met and others I’m still working on, like my smile. I want a better one and I want it soon!

Improving my smile is important to me and I am really excited to be starting a new journey as a spokesperson for Invisalign clear aligners while I am getting treatment for a better smile!

I chose Invisalign clear aligners because they offer discreet teeth straightening without the hassle and embarrassment of traditional wire and bracket braces. I had braces for 4 years when I was a teen, and I can’t imagine wearing them again at this point in my life. Invisalign treatment is a series of virtually invisible, removable aligners that gradually move teeth over time, so hardly anyone even notices when someone is in treatment. With Invisalign clear aligners, an improving smile is on display everyday — no wire or brackets to detract from a confident smile, no interference in professional or personal lives from a mouth full of metal.

Because I’m constantly on the go, I also can’t commit to more visits than are absolutely necessary and Invisalign trained doctors typically provide several sets of clear aligners at each visit, so appointments are kept to a minimum.

I will be starting treatment for my new beautiful smile soon, so please check back in to learn more and follow along on this new adventure with me!

 

Is having a beautiful smile important to you too?

I have partnered with the Invisalign brand, but all opinions expressed are my own. Full disclosure.

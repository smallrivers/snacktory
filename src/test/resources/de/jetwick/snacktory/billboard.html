Listen to Taylor Bennett's 'Broad Shoulders,' Featuring His Big Bro Chance the Rapper

Talent runs in Chance the Rapper’s family. The proof is in his younger brother Taylor Bennett's new track called “Broad Shoulders," which features his older sibling.

Chance the Rapper Covers Kanye West's 'Family Business' in New Video

A sweet, piano-driven melody embraces their bars about family life, partly inspired by Chance's real-life role as dad.   

“It's a very meaningful song to our family and our next generation," the younger Bennett tells Rolling Stone of the track. "Chance is now a father, and in the third verse, I speak about my niece -- his daughter -- so it's a really emotional and compassionate song."

Bennett will be pushing out his first solo album -- also titled Broad Shoulders -- next week, a move that big bro Chance is super proud of. “It's raw to watch him mature as an artist and a man,” he also told RS. “He put everything into this one."

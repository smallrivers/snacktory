          Miley Cyrus refuses to grow up

          And 16 other worst looks that ruined the red carpets this week.
          Lorna Cole - Dec 4

          • Pinterest

          The gals from Orange is the New Black were out in full-force on the red carpet this past week, and it appears that all that time behind bars has impacted their fashion sense (even if the time behind bars wasn’t real). But the real criminal in the fashion department this week is Miley Cyrus, who on two separate occasions proved she’d rather imitate Princess Leia and Dolly Parton than do any growing up herself. Check out all the worst looks and see for yourself:

          Miley Cyrus refuses to grow up

          • Facebook
          • Twitter
          • Pinterest
          • +
          of

          • Helen Mirren

            Don’t tell her (because we honestly fear repercussion), but Helen Mirren looked like a young mother’s fridge covered in this Dolce and Gabbana number featuring prints of children’s drawings. Cute drawings. Ugly dress. Getty Images

          • Cynthia Nixon

            Notice how Cynthia Nixon camouflages with the step and repeat. Surely, as one of the eldest of the Sex and the City crew, she should’ve known better. Also, it looks totally out of season—just because it’s warm, doesn’t mean we deserve to wear summer outfits. Getty Images

          • Ellie Goulding

            Ellie Goulding’s suggestive pin-stripe jumpsuit is just a little too try-hard. When you only put one article of clothing on your person, you’d better make sure it’s a good one. Getty Images

          • Emma Myles

            Another OITNB cast member on the list, and another dessert metaphor. If Laura Prepon’s look was an Oreo, Emma Myles is red velvet cake. This is colour blocking gone wrong, and the black nylons don’t help. Getty Images

          • Jessie J

            It’s outfits like this that give good patterns a bad name. Jessie J’s look is reminiscent of an out-of-date hotel carpet..and not in a cool, retro way. This outfit is definitely in need of a renovation. Getty Images

          • Kate Mulgrew

            In prison, this would look great—like, so much better than those orange jumpsuits—but it’s just not ready for the red carpet. The problem is that she’s a bonafide star, but we just don’t know it. Her outfit is too boxy, with shoulders too square, and a suit too double breasted. And too 80s. Getty Images

          • La La Anthony

            Creative direction for La La Anthony’s recent outfit at a NYC premiere: leftovers wrapped in a small piece of rose gold tinfoil. Nailed it! Getty Images

          • Laura Prepon

            This could be a great dress if it were a solid colour. And the structure isn’t all that bad, but it’s the unfortunate layering of black and white fabric that takes us closer to an Oreo cookie metaphor than we want to be while talking fashion.Getty Images

          • Leona Lewis

            This is a straightforward, open-and-shut case of “oh shucks, I forgot to put on pants today.” There’s the coat and the boots, but where are the pants? This look reminds us of the guards at Buckingham Palace…only, without pants. Getty Images

          • Maggie Gyllenhaal

            We’re scratching our heads, too, wondering why Maggie Gyllenhaal put these clothes on. Hmmmmm. There are better ways to get some spotlight than to show off a little skin and half commit to a trend that’s meant for university students. Getty Images

          • Miley Cyrus

            Thank you, Miley, for making our job so fun. Yes, there’s a new Star Wars film. But last time we checked, Miley, you were not reprising the role of Princess Leia. What’s with the bagels? Getty Images

          • Miley Cyrus

            Oh hello, again Miley Parton. Er, we mean Cyrus. But seriously, this looks like a hand-me-down from Dolly…and Miley wears it well. The only problem: it’s about three decades too late. Getty Images

          • Natasha Lyonne

            This look is positively medieval. As we mentioned last week, the issue with an all black dress like this, is that the wearer looks rather witch-like. At least she went sleeveless, which helps, but the turtleneck collar does not. ORANGE is the new black. Orange! Not black! Getty Images

          • RaeLynn

            Country singer RaeLynn’s outfit was just a bit too deep in the neck line. And the squared-off bottom in white creates a boxy effect that’s not flattering. Getty Images

          • Rose McGowan

            There are many things going wrong here. The whole thing is too abstract, with nothing approaching cohesion. Are those board shorts? And where is the rest of your shirt? Go home and try again. Getty Images

          • Salma Hayek

            This all-white look has a lumpy, homogenous effect on the curvaceous Salma Hayek. The white and gold pairing is on point, but the uniboob is not. Getty Images

          • Zendaya

            Zendaya’s jumpsuit (it’s hard to tell just what it is) may be tempting, but in the end it’s just not consumable. She looks like the kind of hard candy you pick up at your grandparent’s house and then put back….nobody wants that swirl. Getty Images
